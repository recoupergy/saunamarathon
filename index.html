What this is

A field guide to the many ways people sauna-hop.

Around the sauna world, "sauna marathon" gets used for all kinds of heat-hopping adventures: car-assisted sauna orienteering, walkable village crawls, citywide open-sauna days, festival villages, serial ceremonies, and judged Aufguss competitions.

We use "sauna marathon" the way people search for it: as a handy umbrella for sauna crawls, festivals, open-sauna days, and competition formats. SaunaMarathon.com is by SaunaNews, and each listing links back to the organizer or source so you can jump straight to the people running it.

Format taxonomy

How to read the category

01

Orienteering rally

Teams follow a map and try to visit many sauna checkpoints within a set time window. Otepää and Schaffhausen are the anchor examples.

02

Village crawl

Participants move on foot, and sometimes by ferry, through a tight local route of private, shoreline, tent, and mobile saunas.

03

Open-sauna day

Homes, hotels, clubs, and public operators open saunas for one coordinated day, usually with flexible ticketing or host-by-host access.

04

Festival village

Temporary saunas, cold plunges, talks, music, food, and rituals are assembled in one venue or district for public programming.

05

Ceremony competition

Events center on serial sauna rituals, sauna-master performances, or judged Aufguss competition rather than route completion.

Organizer playbook

Start with the format. Respect the brands.

01

Map real sauna capacity

Count benches, changing space, cold-water access, toilets, transit, and host staffing before you promise a route or festival village.

02

Pick the transport mode

Car-assisted, walkable, transit-first, ferry-assisted, and one-site festival formats create different safety and permitting problems.

03

Write your own rules

Event ideas are not copyrightable, but another organizer's copy, maps, photos, marks, and logos are protected expression or brand assets.

04

Publish hygiene and clothing policies

Swimwear, towels, age limits, alcohol, recovery, medical access, and sauna-host authority should be visible before tickets go live.
