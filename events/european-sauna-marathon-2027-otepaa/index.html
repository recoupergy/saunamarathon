16th European Sauna Marathon

Otepää's sauna-marathon weekend returns March 5-6, 2027, with a Friday Sauna Village and a Saturday orienteering race between sauna stops.

Otepää, Estonia March 5-6, 2027 Teams of four
Canonical source: SaunaNews - 16th European Sauna Marathon

The European Sauna Marathon returns to Otepää on March 5 and 6, 2027. SaunaNews lists the event as the 16th edition and identifies it as a major social sauna festival built around two connected pieces: a public Sauna Village on Friday and the main sauna-orienteering race on Saturday.

Friday's programme centers on Otepää's central square, where the event village brings together shared sauna rituals, music, food, and a public-facing sauna atmosphere. Saturday is the marathon proper: teams move between mapped sauna locations and try to complete as much of the route as possible before the finish window closes.

The event format is specific. Teams are made up of four participants, and a car plus driver are required. That puts Otepää in the sauna-orienteering branch of the category rather than the walkable village-crawl or one-site festival-village branch used by other events.

Event Details

  • Date: March 5-6, 2027
  • Location: Otepää Central Square and Tehvandi Sports Hall, Estonia
  • Format: Friday Sauna Village, Saturday sauna-orienteering marathon
  • Team structure: Four participants with car and driver
  • Organizer link: saunamaraton.com/en/home

For SaunaMarathon.com, Otepää matters because it is the export model many English-language readers now associate with the phrase "sauna marathon": teams, route strategy, mapped checkpoints, costumes, and a large winter gathering around sauna culture.
