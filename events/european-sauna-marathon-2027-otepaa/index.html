European Sauna Marathon 2027

Otepää's benchmark sauna marathon returns March 5-6, 2027, with a Friday Sauna Village and a Saturday route race between sauna stops.

Otepää, Estonia March 5-6, 2027 Teams of four
Published by SaunaNews: Read the canonical SaunaNews listing

The European Sauna Marathon returns to Otepää on March 5 and 6, 2027. For SaunaMarathon.com, this is the headline example of the route-first format: teams, maps, sauna checkpoints, costumes, logistics, and a winter finish line.

The weekend has two parts. Friday centers on Otepää's central square, where the Sauna Village brings together shared sauna rituals, music, food, and a public gathering point. Saturday is the marathon proper: teams move between mapped sauna locations and try to complete as much of the route as possible before the finish window closes.

The format is specific. Teams are made up of four participants, and a car plus driver are required. That puts Otepää in the sauna-orienteering branch of the sauna-marathon world, separate from the walkable Finnish village routes and distinct from one-site sauna festivals.

Event Details

  • Date: March 5-6, 2027
  • Location: Otepää Central Square and Tehvandi Sports Hall, Estonia
  • Format: Friday Sauna Village, Saturday sauna-orienteering marathon
  • Team structure: Four participants with car and driver
  • Organizer link: saunamaraton.com/en/home

Otepää matters because it gives the phrase "sauna marathon" a concrete shape: route strategy, mapped checkpoints, team identity, many sauna stops, and a large winter gathering around sauna culture.

For registration updates and future corrections, use the SaunaNews article linked above and the official event site. SaunaMarathon.com keeps this guide here so the directory has a clear starting point for the marathon format.
